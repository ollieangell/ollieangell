WE ARE ONE OF DORSET'S LARGEST SMART HOME INSTALLER AND INTEGRATOR

Smart Home is based in Verwood, Dorset, we work with some of the most popular providers and designers to be able to achieve multi-room audio, create and build custom home cinema's and install home lighting controlled from your voice or your phone. We design and install smart home technology to homes all across the UK. We have a passion for music and automation which allows us to build and support projects which exceeds our customers expectations. 

 

Oliver Angell, Smart Homes Managing Director says, 'I've always had a passion for Smart Home Technologies and the improvements over the years, I wanted to make everyone's forever busy life feel easier, it is said that you can save up to £84 per year from turning off one of your lights when your not using it, imagine how much you can save turning your lights off automatically when you leave the room'.
